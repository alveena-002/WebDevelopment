Welcome, Player

You open your eyes. The air is cold, the door is locked, and you have no memory of how you got here. Somewhere in this place are three rooms — each hiding a clue. Read carefully, think wisely, and choose the right path. One wrong choice, and you'll be trapped again.

How to Play

  1. Read the story on each page carefully.
  2. Click details boxes to reveal hidden clues.
  3. Choose a door or chest by clicking its link.
  4. A wrong choice traps you — but you can always try again.
  5. Solve all three rooms to reach the final escape.
